A wide variety of

Keepsakes

We offer a range of memorial options to honor your pet, including customized urns, cremation jewelry, and paw prints. Each private cremation comes with a clay paw print, genuinely crafted from your pet’s own paw, ensuring you have a personal keepsake. These can be displayed in various ways—inside shadowboxes, as ornaments, or alongside your pet’s urn.

For a unique tribute, consider our ceramic paw prints, which are glazed and kiln-fired for durability. If you have a special request, let us know; we're here to help craft the perfect memorial.

Explore our selection of cremation jewelry, featuring necklaces and bracelets that hold a small portion of your pet's cremains, keeping them close at all times. We also collaborate with an independent artist who specializes in creating custom glass pendants with your pet’s cremains, offering a beautiful and lasting way to remember your beloved pet. Visit Henry’s site for more on custom pieces.

Cremation Services
