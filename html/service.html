A History of Compassion

About Us

We are proud to be locally owned! For nearly the last 30 years, Bluegrass Pet Crematorium has been independently owned, managed, and staffed by lifelong animal lovers - we understand the beautiful bond you share with your pets, because we share the same bonds with our own as well. We take pride in the fact that all our services take place at our facility in Lexington, Kentucky as we know how important it is to the families that we serve that their pets remain close to home for their final care.

Our Mission

Our mission at Bluegrass Pet Crematorium is to provide compassionate, respectful final care for pets in a facility that reflects the love and dedication pet owners feel. By maintaining a locally owned and operated space in Lexington, Kentucky, we ensure that every service is performed with utmost integrity and closeness, upholding our commitment to keep your cherished pets near to home throughout their final journey. We strive to offer a comforting experience that honors the unique bond between pets and their families.
