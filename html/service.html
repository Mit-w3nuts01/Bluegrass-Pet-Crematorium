Private Cremation

Private Cremation is our most requested service, and the one we perform most often. Your beloved pet is cremated privately in the cremation chamber, and their cremains are returned to you in the urn you have selected along with any additional keepsakes.

Standard

  • Privately Cremated
  • Cremation Certificate
  • Handmade Wooden Urn
  • 1 Ceramic Paw Print
  • Grief Pamphlet

Deluxe

  • Privately Cremated
  • Cremation Certificate
  • Wooden Photo Urn
  • Brass Nameplate
  • 1 Ceramic Paw Print
  • Grief Pamphlet

Communal Cremation

Communal cremation is only done upon request. On occasion families do not wish to keep their pet's cremains. Instead, they can opt for a communal cremation with or without a paw print. Once the animals are cremated, their ashes are spread at a beautiful horse farm in Fayette County, Kentucky. Communal cremation is when multiple animal's are cremated together; the remains of your companion will not be returned to you.
